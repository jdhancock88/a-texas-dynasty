Spanish Cay

Spanish Cay is a 2½-mile long island 190 miles northeast of Miami. In 1952, it was purchased by Clint Murchison Jr. It had six beaches, a storm-safe harbor, a runway, a coconut grove and a 14,000-square-foot mid-century modern house designed by Murchison.
